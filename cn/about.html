Enterprise introduction

Kachan Tech (Shanghai) Co., ltd. based in Shanghai, has been doing business in China mainland since 2011, and the founder Garry has more than 15 years' experiences of fine chemical customization and sourcing service. We have a cooperating lab in Hyderabad, India, and will have another lab with a Canadian chemical doctor in Mumbai,India. In China, we have labs supporting with doctors. We have dedicated 7 staffs to developing overseas marketing since 2019 to do more overseas trading business, and now more than 10 staffs in our Overseas Dept. We have six different testing centers around China to test the Spectrum analysis before the shipment according to customers' needs, such as C-NMR, H-NMR, HPLC, GC, GC-MS and LC-MS. Meanwhile, for logistics, we gain all varieties of shipping forwarders to speed up the shipment for customers among China, including FedEx, DHL, air and sea shipping to ensure the goods reached to our customers worldwide at the first time. Our website www.Kachanchem.com is still built for overseas customers. and we will add more info then Any enquries, In addition to professional procurement and sales teams and customized laboratories, kachan Chemical also has its own professional logistics team, which can reduce customers' international logistics costs and help different customers solve customs clearance problems.
